What is Synk?

Synk allows applications you use to synchronize read/unread status of items with a centralized service. By itself, this may not be terribly useful, unless you use Synk-enabled applications from multiple computers, such as an RSS reader that you use at home and at work.

How do I get it?

Registering for Synk is a snap — it takes a total of about 3 seconds. Just choose a username and password, then enter it in your Synk-enabled applications on each of your computers, and the rest works like magic.
